Negroni

Cocktail Description

The negroni is a classic cocktail loved by many and hated by others. The drink displays a delicate balance between the bitterness of the gin and Campari, and the sweetness of the Campari and vermouth. The Campari containing both of the primary flavors of the cocktail serves to bridge the gap between the gin and vermouth, locking the flavors together into one cohesive experience.

There are many different variations on the negroni, but at it's most basic and pure it is a simple equal parts recipe of three ingredients. a notable variation is the boulevardier, which swaps the primary spirit to either bourbon or rye depending on who you're asking.

Ingredients

  • 1oz London Dry Gin
  • 1oz Campari
  • 1oz Sweet Vermouth (Pref. Carpano Antica Formula)
  • Orange peel (optional garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add large rock of ice to rocks glass
  2. Combine gin, Campari, and vermouth in glass
  3. Stir ingredients and ice for 30-45 seconds. Vary time to achieve preferred dilution.
  4. Express oils of orange peel into glass and wipe on rim. Add peel to class or discard.
  5. Enjoy!
