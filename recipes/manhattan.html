Manhattan

Cocktail Description

The Manhattan is a cocktail that in the minds of many conjures images of the New York City borough that is it's namesake. The association with the location gives the cocktail a percieved air of sophistication and wealth, after all it is named after the home of Wall Street.

The Manhattan is a simple cocktail of three ingredients, bourbon, sweet vermouth, and traditionally Angostra Bitters. Often the drink will be served with a cherry, and some prefer to substitute rye for the bourbon. A popular version of this recipe can be remembered easily as the amounts of each ingredient correspond to the Manhattan area code, 212.

Ingredients

  • 2oz Bourbon or Rye
  • 1oz Sweet Vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura Bitters(or to taste)
  • 1 Cocktail Cherry(Pref. Luxardo, optional garnish)

Instructions

  1. Add large rock of ice to mixing glass
  2. Add bourbon/rye, vermouth, and bitters to mixing glass
  3. Stir for 30-45 seconds. Vary time to achieve preferred dilution.
  4. Strain into chilled coupe glass
  5. Add cherry to class
  6. Enjoy!
